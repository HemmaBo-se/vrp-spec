Verify it yourself

Don't trust. Verify.

VRP removed the gatekeeper. So prove it: verify a signed stay offer right here, in your own browser.

This page runs the signature check with your browser's native Web Crypto Ed25519. It loads no crypto library, and HemmaBo's servers play no part in the verification. The offer is signed by the host's own domain key — that is the only authority involved.

1. Pick what to verify

Fetches https://<host>/api/verified-stay-offer and /.well-known/jwks.json directly from the host, then verifies locally.

The host's public key is fetched from the canonical_domain inside the offer's /.well-known/jwks.json. Nothing is sent anywhere — the fetch goes straight to the host.
